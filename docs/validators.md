# Validators

## Purpose

Validators help assess the quality, availability, provenance, and consistency of state.

They support the Proof of Coherence model.

## Validator Types

### State Validators

Evaluate state structure and consistency.

### Provenance Validators

Evaluate signatures, hashes, references, and custody records.

### Repair Validators

Evaluate reconciliation events and repair operations.

### Availability Validators

Verify that state records remain accessible and auditable.

### Observer Validators

Provide telemetry regarding propagation, synchronization, and route quality.

## Responsibilities

Validators should:

- verify claims
- record observations
- expose audit information
- avoid hidden state
- document assumptions

## Future Work

Future specifications should define:

- validator scoring
- dispute handling
- quorum requirements
- governance interaction
- operational requirements
